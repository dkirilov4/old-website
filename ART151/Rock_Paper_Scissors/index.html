Rock-Paper-Scissors + Machine Learning

Instructions:

1. Place your hand inside the orange box, and create a gesture you want to train (rock, paper, or scissors) the neural network on. Avoid having other parts of your body in the frame.

2. With your hand inside the box, click on the corresponding gesture which you are making on the left. This provides the network an example to look at.

3. Provide examples of each gesture at least 30 times. Once done, click the "Train" button. After it's done training, click "Predict"

4. You are now ready to play! Click "Shoot" and you will have a 3 second countdown timer to shoot the hand you want to play.
